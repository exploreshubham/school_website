School Facilities

School is the place where children learn, grow, and develop into responsible
individuals. To ensure that students receive a holistic education, schools must
provide basic facilities that cater to the overall development of a child.

School Bus

S.M.C.A. provides safe and reliable air-conditioned bus transportation to students who wish to avail this facility.

The school has a luxury & air-conditioned fleet of buses with the best of safety arrangements available for the neighbouring areas.

Smart Classes

All the classrooms are equipped with audio visual digital facilities. The classrooms of the college are smart in the true sense of the word. The classrooms are equipped with interactive smart boards and projectors.

Healthcare

We believe in the importance of healthcare. To DPS healthcare isn't limited to physical health but emotional as well. to develop an all round presonality of an individual enough emphasis must be given to their emotional & mental development so as to secure their future.

Positive Learning

Director Message

S.M. Children Academy is a school that aims at the complete development of the student. Our staff are handpicked & trained to ensure that the students are given every possible support in all their endeavours academic or otherwise. It is multi-disciplinary school and this also ensure that the students are ready access to a wide range of academic material.

Our brand of education does not have narrow horizons. We believe in exposure. Our students are encouraged to widen their knowledge base and study beyond the confines of the syllabus. This is evidenced by the extensive library facilities in the school. In this age of ARVIND GUPTA - Director - S.M. Children Academy, knowledge is just a click away and we ensure that every student of the school has only the best of technology at his/her disposal.

A student of S.M. Children Academy will lack nothing in terms of support from the management and staff. We aimed to lend a gentle guiding hand so that our student recognize their inner potential and grow of their on accord in to stalwarts of tomorrow's society. An alumni of S.M. Children Academy is sure to be knowledgeable, confident and fully equipped to deal with the real world and excel.

School Classes

S.M.C.A venture is into a complete preschooling sector for children in the age of 2 to
5 years. The S.M.C.A playschool caters to the children in the toddler Playgroup,
Nursery, Lower Kindergarten (LKG), as well as Upper Kindergarten (UKG). These
programs differ from level to level and program-based activities are conducted to
develop the skills of children in the playgroup sector.
